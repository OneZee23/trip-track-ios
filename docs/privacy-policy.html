Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

TripTrack is a personal trip diary app that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All your data — trips, GPS routes, photos, settings, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using CoreData. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Location Data

TripTrack uses your device's GPS to record trip routes. Specifically:

  • Location data is collected only during active trip recording initiated by you.
  • Background location access is used solely to continue recording when the app is in the background.
  • All location data (coordinates, speed, altitude) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted anywhere.
  • Reverse geocoding (converting coordinates to place names) is performed by Apple's built-in MapKit services — no third-party geocoding is used.
  • You can delete any trip and its associated data at any time from within the app.

Photos

Photos you attach to trips are stored locally in the app's Documents directory on your device. They are not uploaded to any server or cloud service.

Analytics & Tracking

TripTrack does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any telemetry data.

Third-Party Services

TripTrack does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data. The app is built entirely with native Apple frameworks.

Children's Privacy

TripTrack does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
